## Support

**Bug reports & app questions**  
Please [open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/c3rooks/Slay_The_Spire2_Companion/issues/new). Include:

- iPhone model and **iOS version**
- What you were doing and what went wrong (screenshots help)

**In-app purchases (Pro characters, tips)**  
Use **Settings → [your Apple ID] → Media & Purchases** or the **Report a Problem** link on your App Store receipt. Refunds and payment issues are handled by **Apple**; developers cannot process refunds from this page.

**Privacy**  
The app runs on your device. See the **App Privacy** labels on the App Store listing for details.
